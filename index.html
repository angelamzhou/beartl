The money is almost gone. Cicero wants out. A storm is rolling in off the lake. You have twelve days to run this kitchen through every service, every disaster, and every Fak — and finish with one perfect night when it matters most.

An FTL-style kitchen roguelike. Assign your brigade to stations, clear tickets before diners walk, fight fires, keep the dish pit moving, and protect your REPUTATION — when it hits zero, The Bear closes for good.

⏸ SPACE pauses time (use it constantly — every second counts). Click a chef, then click a station to move them. EXPO plates finished tickets: nothing leaves the kitchen without it.
